Research

My research interests lie in the application of Machine Learning to interdisciplinary domains, such as healthcare, as well as multimedia data, specifically videos.

Specifically, I am highly motivated to pursue the following three areas of research:

1) the construction of algorithms for video understanding that can effectively learn multi-modal representations.

2) the development of resilient learning systems that can contribute to the advancement of interdisciplinary fields in artificial intelligence and medical image analysis.

3) the design of multi-modal learning algorithms that can seamlessly integrate biomedical data, with the ultimate goal of improving healthcare outcomes.
